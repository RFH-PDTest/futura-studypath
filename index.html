For Teachers & Counselors

Futura flips the script on chronic absenteeism by giving students a clear "why" for attending class. Monitor progress from the teacher dashboard.

Evidence-based Approach

Research shows that chronic absenteeism is strongly correlated with a lack of connection between schoolwork and future goals. Futura addresses this through career-mapped engagement, AI-powered catch-up to prevent "falling behind" spirals, and calendar-integrated study planning.
